Official Slogan

Brand taglines and messaging framework

Primary Slogan
"Detecting the pulse of music trends"
Our primary tagline that captures the essence of real-time music trend detection and analysis. Emphasizes the dynamic, living nature of music data.
Use for: Hero sections, main marketing materials, primary brand messaging
"Real-time music trend intelligence"
Technical tagline emphasizing the real-time data processing and intelligent analytics capabilities of the platform.
Use for: Developer documentation, API marketing, technical audiences
"Data-driven music discovery"
Focuses on the discovery aspect, highlighting how data powers better music recommendations and trend forecasting.
Use for: Product features, user-facing applications, discovery tools
"The future of music analytics"
Visionary tagline positioning trendradar as the industry leader in music analytics and trend prediction technology.
Use for: Press releases, investor materials, vision statements
"By developers, for developers"
Community-focused tagline emphasizing our developer-first approach and commitment to the developer community.
Use for: Developer community, open-source projects, hackathons
Slogan Usage Guidelines
Primary Placement
Always place slogan below or next to the logo with appropriate whitespace. Minimum distance: 1x logo height.
Typography
Use Plus Jakarta Sans (Regular/Medium). Size should be 1/3 to 1/2 of logo text size. Never use Orbitron for slogans.
Color Options
Use Soft White (#F8FAFC) on dark backgrounds, or Dark Gray (#64748B) for subtle branding. Never use gradient on slogan text.
Consistency
Choose one slogan per campaign or context. Don't mix multiple slogans in the same visual space or communication.
